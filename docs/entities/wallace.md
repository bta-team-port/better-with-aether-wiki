## Wallace
Mimic are great, but none are as great as Wallace. Some say that Wallace is a myth, others believe Wallace is real, and yet others believe Wallace is something in between.
Truth be told Wallace is a legend.
Wallace is much stronger and much tougher than normal mimic.
Wallace takes only a third of the damage while dealing 50% more damage of typical mimic.

Wallace can rarely spawn instead mimic in Mazes and Temple.
Wallace is quite rare- The odds are 1:256 for a mimic to be a wallace.
Spotting Wallace is quite easy.
Wallace spots a deep bruting shiny purple polish and a label to boot. 
If you found Wallace let me advise you, as an avid Wallace survivor, run!
